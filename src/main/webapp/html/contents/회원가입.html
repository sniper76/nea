Job Seeker, Student/Intern Registration Process

View larger image
  • After writing your CV, you can register for JC Job Registration in My Page.
  • After JC Job Registration, your CV will be disclosed online, and you can be offered by employers or referred by JC.
  • You should cancel your JC Job Registration if you are employed or stopped your job seeking activities.

Employer Registration Process

View larger image
  • To use the NEA online service, employer should register Job Vacancy then approval from JC.
  • The purpose of the approval is to increase reliability and prevent harm caused by false information.
  • You can register for more than one job vacancies. You can use various services such as online recruitment after approval.
  • Approval on the job vacancy can be tracked in My Page after login.
  • Employer should cancel the job vacancy if you do not need to recruit any more.

Process of Registering Training Program (For Companies)

View larger image
  • To use the NEA online service, registered training program should be approved by JC.
  • The purpose of the approval is to increase reliability and prevent harm caused by false information.
  • Training Institutions can register more than one programs, and it can use various NEA online services after approval by JC.
